Cookies Policy

At TALKS, we value your privacy and are committed to being transparent about how we use cookies. This section explains what cookies are, the different types of cookies we use, how we use them, and how you can manage your cookie preferences to enhance your browsing experience.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit websites. These files are placed by the website you visit to collect information about your browsing behavior, preferences, and interactions with the site. Cookies enable websites to remember you and your preferences, making it easier for you to navigate and interact with the site during future visits. They can enhance the functionality of the site and provide a personalized experience based on your previous actions.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

We use several types of cookies to improve your experience on TALKS. Below is a detailed description of each type of cookie:

  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the basic operation of our website and are crucial for providing you with the services you request. They help in tasks like logging in, completing forms, and ensuring website security. Without these cookies, certain functionalities of TALKS may not be available.
  • Analytical Cookies: These cookies collect information about how visitors use the website, such as the pages viewed, the links clicked, and the time spent on the site. This data helps us analyze website traffic and performance, allowing us to make improvements and offer a better user experience. These cookies are typically anonymous and do not store personal data.
  • Functional Cookies: Functional cookies enable advanced website features that enhance the user experience. They allow us to remember your preferences, such as language settings, and provide customized content. They help ensure that the website responds to your individual needs, offering a more personalized experience each time you visit.
  • Targeting Cookies: These cookies are used to track your browsing behavior across websites and deliver more relevant advertisements or content based on your interests. They help us present you with ads that are tailored to you, improving the overall relevance of the content you see during your browsing sessions.

3. Managing Cookies

You have the ability to manage cookies through your browser settings. Most modern browsers allow you to block or delete cookies from specific sites, or even prevent cookies from being stored entirely. However, please note that disabling cookies may affect the performance of certain features and functionalities of TALKS. You may experience a less personalized user experience, or some parts of the website may not function as intended.

For more detailed information on how to manage your cookies, refer to the help section of your browser. Keep in mind that if you delete or disable cookies, some parts of TALKS may not function properly, and we may not be able to remember your preferences across sessions.

If you have any concerns or questions about how cookies are used on TALKS, feel free to contact our support team for more information.
