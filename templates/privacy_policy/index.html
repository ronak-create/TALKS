1. Data Collection

At TALKS, we collect data to improve your experience on our platform. The data we gather allows us to personalize your interactions, offer relevant content, and enhance the overall functionality of our services. The types of data we collect are divided into the following categories:

  • Personal Information: This includes your name, email address, and other profile details that you provide when creating or updating your account. This information helps us identify and communicate with you effectively.

For a more detailed explanation of what data we collect and how we use it, please visit our Data Collection page.

2. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your experience while using TALKS. Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device to help us remember your preferences, understand your usage patterns, and deliver more relevant content. By using our platform, you consent to the use of cookies, which help us analyze user behavior and improve functionality. Cookies can also be used for marketing purposes to display personalized advertisements based on your interests.

For more details on how we use cookies and how you can manage your preferences, please refer to our Cookies page.
